# kamino-lend
A comprehensive lending protocol plugin that enables users to supply, borrow, and manage positions on Kamino Lend — the leading Solana lending protocol.

## Highlights
- View all lending markets with real-time supply/borrow APYs and TVL
- Check your lending positions and health factor across all obligations
- Supply assets to earn yield on deposits
- Withdraw supplied assets with health factor validation
- Preview and execute borrowing against collateral
- Repay outstanding loans with dry-run capability
- Comprehensive error handling and transaction previews
- Support for major Solana tokens (USDC, SOL) with UI-friendly amounts
