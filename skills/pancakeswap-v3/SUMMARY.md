# pancakeswap-v3
Swap tokens and manage concentrated liquidity on PancakeSwap V3 across BNB Chain, Base, Ethereum, Arbitrum, and Linea.

## Highlights
- Token swaps via SmartRouter with optimal routing across fee tiers
- Concentrated liquidity position management (mint/remove positions)
- Multi-chain support (BNB Chain, Base, Ethereum, Arbitrum, Linea)
- Real-time price quotes without transaction costs
- Automated tick range selection for liquidity provision
- Pool discovery and analysis across all fee tiers
- Position portfolio tracking and management
- Built-in slippage protection and balance validation
